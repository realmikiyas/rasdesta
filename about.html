A Legacy of Healing and Hope

Our History

Ras Desta Damtew Memorial Hospital stands as a proud testament to a legacy of service and patriotism. Established to serve the needs of the Ethiopian people, our hospital is named in honor of Ras Desta Damtew, a distinguished leader and patriot. From our foundational years to the present day, our purpose has remained steadfast: to be a sanctuary of healing, a center for medical advancement, and a pillar of strength for the community of Addis Ababa. Every patient we treat and every student we train is a part of this enduring story.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission Statement:

To improve the health and well-being of the communities we serve by delivering outstanding, patient-centered care; to advance medicine through innovative and locally relevant research; and to cultivate the next generation of healthcare professionals through excellence in education and training.

Vision Statement:

To be Ethiopia's leading academic medical center, recognized for setting the standard in specialized medical care, transformative research, and compassionate service, creating a healthier future for all.

Our Commitment to the Community

Ras Desta Damtew Memorial Hospital is more than just a healthcare provider; we are an integral part of the fabric of Addis Ababa. We are deeply committed to enhancing public health through community outreach programs, health education, and providing accessible care to all who need it. We understand our vital role as a healthcare resource and are dedicated to addressing the specific needs of our region, ensuring we contribute positively to a stronger, healthier Ethiopia.
