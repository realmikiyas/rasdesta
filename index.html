A Legacy of Service

Named in honor of the patriot Ras Desta Damtew, our hospital was founded on a legacy of unwavering service to the Ethiopian people. We continue this legacy today by upholding the highest standards of medical excellence and demonstrating a profound commitment to the health of our community.
