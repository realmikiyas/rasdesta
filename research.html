Advancing Medicine Through Research

Research is a core pillar of our mission. We are committed to fostering a vibrant research environment that addresses the most pressing health issues facing Ethiopia.

Our Primary Areas of Research

  • Antimicrobial Stewardship & Resistance Preventio: Our DUE programs assess antibiotic utilization in hospital , focusing on guideline compliance, de- escalation practices, and resistance patterns. We develop evidence-based interventions to promote optimal antimicrobial use and combat the growing threat of drug-resistant infections.
  • Non comunicable disease:We investigate population-level understanding of major NCDs (diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular diseases, cancer, and chronic respiratory conditions), evaluating knowledge gaps, risk perception, and preventive behaviors. Our research informs targeted health education strategies to improve early detection and self-management.
  • Health care leadership:We examine how organizational culture dimensions (collaboration, innovation, support systems) influence healthcare professionals' intention to leave. Our research identifies cultural predictors of attrition in hospitals, pharmacies, and clinical research settings.
