Advancing Medicine Through Research

Research is a core pillar of our mission. We are committed to fostering a vibrant research environment that addresses the most pressing health issues facing Ethiopia.

Our Primary Areas of Research

  • Infectious Diseases: A significant focus of our research is on tuberculosis (TB), exploring improved diagnostic methods, treatment adherence, and outcomes.
  • Ophthalmology: We investigate risk factors, treatment efficacy, and surgical outcomes for conditions like glaucoma to advance the standard of eye care.
  • Surgical Practices: Our research focuses on improving surgical techniques, enhancing patient safety protocols, and evaluating procedural effectiveness.
  • Ethnobotany & Traditional Medicine: We conduct pioneering research into the efficacy and safety of traditional medicinal plants, bridging traditional knowledge with modern science.

Sample Publications

Our findings are shared with the global medical community through publications in reputable, peer-reviewed medical journals.

  • Assefa, M., et al. (2024). "Treatment Outcomes of Drug-Resistant Tuberculosis at Ras Desta Damtew Memorial Hospital." Ethiopian Journal of Health Sciences.
  • Bekele, T. & Girma, S. (2023). "Prevalence and Risk Factors for Glaucoma in an Urban Ethiopian Population." Journal of Ophthalmology for Eastern Africa.
  • Yilma, Z. (2022). "An Ethnobotanical Study of Medicinal Plants Used in Addis Ababa for Dermatological Conditions." Journal of Ethnopharmacology.
